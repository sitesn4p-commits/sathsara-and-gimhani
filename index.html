Our Journey of Love

Their story began with a spark of magic during the school's World Children’s Day celebration. Amidst the joyful chaos of the day, Gimhani stood out, wearing a beautiful red dress that instantly caught Madusha's eye. That fleeting glance became the foundation of a beautiful connection.

Their romantic journey officially blossomed on October 15, 2022. It was a time of innocent youth; Madusha was in his final term of Grade 10, and Gimhani was finishing Grade 7. As they grew up together, their love matured with them.

Despite facing various challenges and obstacles along the way, their bond remained unbroken and only grew stronger. Now, years later, their love has stood the ultimate test of time. We are overjoyed to announce that they will officially tie the knot on June 15, 2036.
